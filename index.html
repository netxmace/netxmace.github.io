Tech Facts

- Apple's WWDC 2024: Apple's Worldwide Developers Conference will be held online from June 10 to 14, showcasing new advancements in iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, tvOS, and visionOS​.

- The first webcam was deployed at Cambridge University in 1991 to monitor the status of a coffee pot. It allowed researchers to see if there was coffee in the pot before making the trip to refill it.

- The average smartphone today has more computing power than the computers used for the Apollo 11 moon landing in 1969.

- The first computer virus was created in 1983 by a 15-year-old programmer named Rich Skrenta. It was called the "Elk Cloner" and targeted Apple II computers.
