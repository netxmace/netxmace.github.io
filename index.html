Tech Facts

- In late 2019, Google claimed to achieve quantum supremacy, demonstrating a quantum computer capable of solving a problem that would take the world's fastest supercomputer thousands of years to solve. This breakthrough opens the door to solving complex problems in cryptography, material science, and drug discovery.

- SpaceX, led by Elon Musk, has launched thousands of small satellites as part of its Starlink project, aiming to provide high-speed internet access to underserved areas globally. This constellation of satellites has sparked excitement and debate about the future of satellite internet and its impact on telecommunications
