Tech Facts

- Apple's WWDC 2024: Apple's Worldwide Developers Conference will be held online from June 10 to 14, showcasing new advancements in iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, tvOS, and visionOS​.

- Intel's New SoC: At Computex 2024, Intel unveiled a new system-on-a-chip with 4x increased NPU processing power and a 50% faster GPU based on its next-gen Arc Battlemage architecture​

- Nvidia's Automatic GPU Overclocking: Nvidia introduced a feature allowing automatic overclocking of RTX GPUs without voiding warranties, enhancing gaming performance safely​
